Privacy Policy for QuizKar

Effective Date: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in QuizKar ("the App"). The App does not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in the QuizKar application natively.

1. Information We Collect

QuizKar is designed to operate mostly locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account, nor do we collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2. Third-Party Services and Advertisements

While the App itself does not collect personal information, it uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, primarily for serving advertisements and generating content:

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may use advertising IDs (such as the Android Advertising ID) and cookies to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites or apps that use their services by visiting the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API: The App uses the Gemini API to generate custom quiz questions dynamically. When you request an AI-generated quiz, the topic you enter (and any images you choose to upload for context) are sent to the Google Gemini API solely for the purpose of generating the quiz content.

3. Local Storage

All of your quiz progress, generated topics, history, and scores are saved locally on your device's internal storage and are not transmitted to any external servers.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the developer.
